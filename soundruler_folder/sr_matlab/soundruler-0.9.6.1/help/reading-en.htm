Suggested Reading

     This is not intended to be a "best of all" or a complete bibliographic list, just a good starting point.

     Beranek, L. L. 1986. 2nd. Acoustics. Acoustical Society of America.
     Ewing, A. W. 1989. Arthropod bioacoustics - neurobiology and behavior. Cornell University Press.
     Fletcher, N. H. 1992. Acoustic systems in biology. Oxford University Press.
     Gerhardt, H. C. and Huber, F. 2002. Acoustic Communication in insects and anurans. University of Chicago Press.
     Littlejohn, M. J. 2001. Patterns of differentiation in temporal properties of acoustic signals in anurans. In: Anuran Communication. M. Ryan (ed.). Smithsonian Institution Press.
     Hassall, J. R. and Zaveri, K. 1979. 4th. Acoustic Noise Measurements. Bruel & Kjaer.
     Hartmann, W. M. 1996. Signals, sound and sensation - modern acoustics and signal processing. Springer-Verlag.
     Kroodsma, D. E. and Miller, E. H. (eds.). 1982. Vol 1. Acoustic communication in birds. Academic Press, New York.
     Oppenheim, A. V. and Schafer, R. W. 1975. Digital signal processing. Prentice-Hall.
     Oppenheim, A. V. and Schafer, R. W. 1989. Discrete-time signal processing. Prentice-Hall.
     Rossing, T. D. 1990. The science of sound. 2nd. Addison-Wesley Publishing Company.
     Vehrencamp, S. L. and Bradbury, J. W. 1998. Principles of animal communication. Sinauer Associates.
