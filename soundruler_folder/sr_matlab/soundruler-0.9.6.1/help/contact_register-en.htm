Contact & Registration

     Would you like to have a better working Sound Ruler?
     Then please help me justify developing it.
     Send an e-mail to mgpapp@users.sourceforge.net with:

  • Name
  • Locality (City, state, country) and institution
  • Use that you are making of Sound Ruler
  • One suggestion for improvement

     Note: Registration is free. I will not send you unsolicited email and will keep your address confidential.
     You will receive a short note when a new version is out, and you can stop it by answering with Unsubscribe.
